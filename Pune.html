Aga Khan Palace

The Aga Khan Palace was built by Sultan Muhammed Shah Aga Khan III in the city of Pune, India.

Laal Mahal

❮ ❯

The Lal Mahal of Pune is one of the most famous monuments located in Pune, India, where Shivaji I, founder of the Maratha Empire, spent his childhood.

Top Attractions

Shrimant Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati is the epitome of pride and honor to the city of Pune.

ISKCON Pune is a Gaudiya Vaishnavism temple, the temple is dedicated to Hindu god Radha Krishna and was opened in 2013.

Commonly known as the Rajiv Gandhi Zoo or Katraj Zoo, is located in Katraj. It is managed by the Pune Municipal Corporation.

Sinhghad fort is a treat for history enthusiasts, nature lovers as well as foodies

Tamhini Ghat is noted for its surroundings comprising scenic waterfalls, lakes and dense woods.

Khadakwasla Dam is a dam on the Mutha River 21 km from the centre of the city of Pune in Maharashtra, India.
